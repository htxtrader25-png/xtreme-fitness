Got Questions
FAQ
Do I need to be in shape to join?
−
Absolutely not. This bootcamp is designed for all fitness levels — beginner to advanced. Coach Anthony will offer modifications for every exercise so you can work at your own pace and still get a killer workout.
What do I need to bring?
+
Just yourself, water, and a towel. All light weights and resistance bands are provided. Wear comfortable workout clothes and athletic shoes.
Where exactly is Caradine Park?
+
Caradine Park is located in Jasper, TX. Exact meeting point and parking details will be sent in your confirmation email after registration.
When do I need to pay?
+
Payment via Zelle (xfitness@gmail.com / 409-383-7611), Cash App ($xfitness), Venmo (@xfitness), or cash is due before April 30. Cash can be paid in person on Day 1 at 6:00 AM. Your spot is reserved once you register.
What if I miss a day?
+
Each session stands on its own, so missing one day won't stop you from participating in the others. We encourage you to make all 4 sessions for the full experience.
Is there a refund policy?
+
Registrations are non-refundable, but your spot may be transferred to someone else. Contact us before April 25 to arrange a transfer.
